ℹ️ About

The 2026 World Cup runs June 11 – July 19, 2026 in the USA, Mexico and Canada. 48 teams, 12 groups (A–L). The top 2 from each group + the 8 best third-placed teams advance to the round of 32.

✅ The draw and group-stage schedule (72 matches) are official. The player list includes each team's key stars (not a full 26-man squad), and stats (goals/assists) start at zero — update them yourself after each round, or import from an API.
